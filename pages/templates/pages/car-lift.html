BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

PLATFORM SIZE

Shaft Dimensions

Minimum shaft size of 3200×6200 mm for standard car platforms. Larger shaft required for SUVs and vans (3500×6500 mm). Custom dimensions available for all vehicle types.

HEADROOM

Overhead Clearance

Minimum clear cabin height of 2200 mm required. Overhead clearance of 5000–6000 mm above top floor needed. Basement pits of 1200–1600 mm required depending on drive type.

POWER

Three-Phase Required

Three-phase 415V, 50Hz supply mandatory for hydraulic power units. Motor rating of 7.5–22 KW depending on capacity. Separate feeder circuit recommended.

CIVIL

Structural Reinforcement

The pit and shaft walls must be RCC with structural engineer's clearance for car loads. Loading area floor must bear minimum 500 KG/m². We provide detailed structural load data.

ENTRY DOOR

Wide Door Opening

Minimum door width of 2800 mm and height of 2200 mm required. Power bi-folding or horizontal rolling doors recommended. Fire-rated doors available for enclosed parking.

COMPLIANCE

Safety Standards

Meets NBC 2016, IS 14665 Part 5, local municipal fire NOC and factory/parking regulations. We provide all required inspection certificates and operational permits.
