BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

SHAFT SPACE

Open Atrium / Lobby

Requires an open atrium or lobby area with sufficient overhead height. The shaft diameter is 1800–2400 mm for standard capsule models. No brick shaft needed — steel frame is self-supporting.

LOCATION

Indoor or Outdoor

Available for indoor atriums, outdoor spaces and building façades. Outdoor models come with UV-resistant laminated glass and weatherproof stainless steel frames with IP55 protection.

POWER

Three-Phase Supply

Three-phase 415V supply required. LED shaft lighting runs on a separate 230V circuit for independent operation. Backup supply recommended for hotel and mall installations.

CIVIL

Foundation & Pit

Pit depth of 800–1200 mm required. A reinforced concrete foundation pad is needed to anchor the freestanding shaft structure safely, especially for outdoor and atrium models.

HEIGHT

Overhead Clearance

Overhead clearance of 3800–4200 mm required. For outdoor models, wind load and seismic calculations are performed as part of the structural design process.

DESIGN

Architectural Planning

Our design team works with your architect during the planning stage to integrate the capsule lift seamlessly into building aesthetics, lighting design and access routes.
