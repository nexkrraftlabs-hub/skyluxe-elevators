BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

SHAFT SIZE

Custom Shaft Dimensions

Shaft size is fully customised based on your cabin requirement. Standard cabins range from 1500×2000 mm to 3000×4000 mm. RCC or steel shaft both acceptable.

DOOR

Wide Opening Required

Minimum door opening of 1500 mm for pallet truck access. Up to 3000 mm for forklift entry models. Options include manual collapsible gates and power bi-parting doors.

POWER

Three-Phase Supply

Three-phase 415V, 50Hz supply mandatory for motors above 7.5 HP. VFD controller reduces starting current surge and improves energy efficiency during operation.

LOAD

Capacity Planning

We recommend calculating based on max single-trip load plus 25% safety margin. Capacities from 500 KG to 10,000 KG available — we help you choose the right tonnage.

CIVIL

Pit & Floor Reinforcement

Pit depth of 600–1200 mm depending on capacity. Loading area flooring must be reinforced for heavy loads. Buffer stops and wheel guides recommended at entry points.

COMPLIANCE

Industrial Standards

Compliant with IS 14665 Part 5 (Service Lifts), Factory Act regulations and local municipal authority requirements. We obtain all necessary operational permits.
