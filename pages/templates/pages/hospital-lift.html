BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

SHAFT SIZE

Wide Shaft Needed

Minimum shaft size of 2100×2400 mm to accommodate a stretcher-compatible cabin. Wider shaft for dual-door configurations serving OT and ICU floors.

DOOR

Wide Door Opening

Minimum 1100 mm clear door opening required for standard hospital stretchers. 1400 mm opening for wide hospital beds. Auto sliding 2/4 panel door recommended.

POWER

Dedicated Supply

Three-phase 415V dedicated circuit recommended. UPS / DG backup integration mandatory for uninterrupted operation during power failures in critical areas.

LOAD

Heavy-Duty Capacity

Minimum 1000 KG recommended for general hospital use. 2000–2500 KG models for ICU patient beds, medical trolleys and equipment transport.

CIVIL

Pit & Overhead

Pit depth of 1400–1600 mm and overhead clearance of 4200 mm required. RCC shaft construction with smooth landing accuracy of ±5 mm essential.

COMPLIANCE

Hospital Standards

Meets IS 14665, NBC 2016, NABH and fire safety codes. Braille buttons, voice announcement and handrails on three walls meet disability accessibility norms.
