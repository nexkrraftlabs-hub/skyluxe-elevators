BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

SPACE

Shaft Dimensions

Minimum shaft size of 1600×1800 mm for standard 8-person cabin. Larger configurations available. MRL (Machine Room Less) models save additional space.

POWER

Three-Phase Supply

Three-phase 415V, 50Hz supply required. VVVF drive reduces peak current draw. Energy regeneration feeds surplus power back to building grid.

CIVIL

Pit & Overhead

Pit depth of 1200–1500 mm and overhead clearance of 4200–4500 mm required. RCC shaft construction recommended for commercial installations.

CAPACITY

Persons per Trip

Standard models carry 8 to 20 persons per trip (480 KG to 2000 KG). We offer capacity calculations based on your building's peak-hour footfall.

FLOORS

Floor Coverage

Serves up to 30 floors with speed options from 1.0 m/s (low-rise) to 2.5 m/s (high-rise). Destination control available for 15+ floor buildings.

COMPLIANCE

Standards Met

Fully compliant with IS 14665, NBC 2016, EN 81-20/50 and BIS standards. We handle all statutory approvals and inspection certifications.
