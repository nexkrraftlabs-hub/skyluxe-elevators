BEFORE INSTALLATION

Site Requirements

SHAFT SIZE

Shaft Dimensions

Minimum shaft size of 1500×1600 mm for standard 8-person cabin. Standard dimensions for a geared system require slightly more overhead space for the machine room above the shaft.

MACHINE ROOM

Overhead Room Required

A machine room of approx. 8–12 sq m is required above the top floor. This houses the drive motor and control panel. Our team provides detailed machine room layout plans.

POWER

Three-Phase Supply

Three-phase 415V, 50Hz supply required in the machine room. VVVF drive controller reduces peak current and ensures smooth, energy-efficient motor operation.

CIVIL

Pit & Overhead

Pit depth of 1000–1200 mm and overhead clearance of 3800–4200 mm required. RCC shaft construction recommended for all commercial installations.

CAPACITY

Persons per Trip

Standard models carry 10 to 16 persons per trip (1000 KG to 1600 KG). Ideal for office buildings, apartments, hotels and commercial complexes up to 8 floors.

COMPLIANCE

Standards Met

Fully compliant with IS 14665, NBC 2016 and BIS standards. We handle all statutory approvals, chief inspector certifications and operational permits.
